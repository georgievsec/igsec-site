Our Services

Web Application Penetration Testing

We identify vulnerabilities in your web applications using a combination of manual testing and automated scanning. Typical findings include XSS, SQL Injection, CSRF, SSRF, authentication flaws, and more. You'll receive a detailed report with remediation guidance.

Incident Response & Investigation

Experienced a breach or suspicious activity? We provide rapid response to isolate threats, identify the entry point, and guide you through the recovery. We help you understand what happened, how to mitigate it, and how to prevent recurrence.

Post-Incident Forensics

After the immediate threat is resolved, we perform forensic analysis to reconstruct the timeline, analyze attacker behavior, extract deleted evidence, and assess the overall damage. Our forensic reports are suitable for internal use or legal proceedings.
